A hexadecimal is a 6 digit representation of a color. The first two digits(RR) represent a red value, the next two are a green value(GG), and the last are the blue value(BB). A hexadecimal value can be taken from any graphics software like Adobe Photoshop, Paintshop Pro or MS Paint. Each hexadecimal code will be preceded by a pound or hash sign #.
